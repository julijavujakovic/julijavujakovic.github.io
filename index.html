About Me

A few things
worth knowing.

I am a multidisciplinary data scientist with an MBA in Data Analytics and a background in psychology, which shapes how I approach every problem. Most analysts can tell you what the data says. The psychology training helps me understand why people make the decisions they do, and how to present findings in a way that actually lands. I am also a former NCAA Division I team captain and played for the Luxembourg women's national basketball team for eight years, which has everything to do with how I work. I show up, I lead, and I do not drop things when it gets hard.

Growing up in Luxembourg and speaking five languages has made me genuinely good at stepping into new environments quickly. Whether that is a new client, a new industry, or a new team, I assimilate fast and get to work. That is the thing I hear most consistently from the people I have worked with.

I am particularly drawn to roles in healthcare, mental health, and tech, spaces where data has a direct impact on people, not just bottom lines. My psychology background gives me a different lens on these industries than most analysts bring to the table.
